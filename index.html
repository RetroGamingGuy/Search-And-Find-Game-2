Spot The Differences at Delview

Instructions

  • There are 9 levels to play through.
  • Each level will have 2 images.
  • The top image is the original, while the bottom has something changed.
  • Find and click the difference on the bottom image to move on to the next image.
  • There is only one difference per level.
  • Each image will get more harder at spotting the differences, so good luck.

Click the play button to start the game.
